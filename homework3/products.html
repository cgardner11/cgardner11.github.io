ABC Toys Products

ABC Toys is a company that opened its doors in 2001 and it has kept its mission for all those years.

Looking for a playground of fun educational toys for your kids? Well, jump in and take and look around at our cool, unique toys that will make playtime both fun and educational!

Max Robo

Max Robot is the perfect way to teach your child their ABC's, numbers, colors and more!

Take Max anywhere! A fun voice changer that records your childs voice or any special sound or message, this interactive toy is great for both young kids or toddlers.

Fun for traveling in a car or plane. Made with a durable, hard-plastic body with moveable parts.

Ditto & Farly Moonwalker

Ditto and Farly Moonwalker are your childs' astronomy teachers. Push-button controls that teach your child fun facts about space, like stars, planets, the moon etc.

Push and roll this moonwalker around wherever you go!

Drexie Starlight

Snuggle up with Drexie, a cozy best friend that sings to your child with over 25 different lullabies.

A internal voice recorder records any custom song as well. Capture your sweet childs voice forever. Made with a super-soft, durable, stain-resistant, plush fabric. Recording unit removes easily from back compartment for safe washing.

Astro-Rocketeer

Astro-Rocketeer walks, talks, sings and helps your childs imagination soar into the atmosphere!

Fun moveable parts with push button laser-flash hands that keep you exploring into the night. Astro can also be removed from his rocket carrier, just lift his lunar shield and you can take him anywhere you go!

This unique toy is a space wonder for all kids ages 3 and up.

Gero Robo Reader

Gero's great at storytime! He comes with 25 exciting short stories. Want more? There's a huge assortment with all kinds of topics, stories, learning tools for download available online (easy bluetooth download link), 2GB memory capacity.

Gero not only reads, but can be a fun way to teach your child their ABC's, numbers, colors..whatever you want!
